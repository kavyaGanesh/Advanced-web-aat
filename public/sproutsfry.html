    SPROUTS FRY


    INGREDIENTS:

    Servings: 2-3 persons

    1.Bean sprouts - 3 Cups
    2.Clove garlic - 2
    3.Soy sauce - 2 teaspoons
    4.Vegetable oil - 1 teaspoon


    DESCRIPTION:

    1.Heat up 1 teaspoon of vegetable oil in a frying pan. Meanwhile, mince two cloves of garlic.
    2. When the oil is hot enough, fry the minced garlic over medium heat. Meanwhile, rinse and prepare 3 cups of bean sprouts.
    3. When the garlic starts to brown, throw in the bean sprouts and stir-fry over medium to high heat for about 2-3 minutes.
    4. Towards the end, add 2 teaspoons of soy sauce and fry for about another minute. Turn off the heat and serve.


    HEALTHY TIPS:

    1. Sprouts have omega-3 fatty acids which help in boosting good cholesterol (HDL) levels
    2. The presence of vitamin A in sprouts stimulates hair follicles and encourages the scalp to grow thicker and longer hair.
