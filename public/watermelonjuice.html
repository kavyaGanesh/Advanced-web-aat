    WATERMELON JUICE


    INGREDIENTS:

    1. 4 cups Watermelon cubed
    2.Tablespoons Lemon juice or lime juice
    3.Salt to taste
    4.Honey or sugar to taste, optional


    STEPS TO MAKE:

    1. Chop the watermelon without rind into big chunks..
    2. Remove the seeds if any. It is advisable to use seedless watermelon. Also always use chilled watermelon to make juice.
    3. take it to the blender, add lemon juice, salt and sugar. My watermelon was sweet, so I skipped the sugar.
    4. Blend it till smooth and totally pureed.
    5. Remove it to a serving glass.
    6. If needed you can add ice cubes in the glass as well.



    HEALTHY TIPS:

      1.watermelon is particularly high in vitamin C, as well as having more than enough beta-carotene that could be converted into vitamin A in your body if needed.
      2.A big glass of watermelon juice before heading to the beach could provide a little extra antioxidant protection against ultraviolet light on your skin.
