    HOMEMADE CERELAC


    INGREDIENTS:

    AGE- 5 months and above babies

    Brown rice – 1 cup
    Ragi – 1 cup
    Horsegram – ½ cup
    Masoor dal – ½ cup
    Moong dal – ½ cup
    Almonds – ½ cup
    Elaichi – 8-10 pods


    DESCRIPTION:

    1.Ragi/ Nachni, horse gram, masoor dal, and moong dal can be soaked overnight separately in different containers.
    2. Tie the grains in a soft cotton cloth separately and keep them aside at a dry place.
    3.Dry the grains for a day.
    4.Roast brown rice, ragi/ nachni, horse gram, masoor dal, moong dal, almonds and elaichi in a clean and dry non-stick vessel separately. Make sure each ingredient is roasted on a medium-low flame to ensure that it’s cooked well from the inside and not just outside. Once roasted, the colour becomes darker. Keep aside to cool..
    5.Once cooled, transfer the mixture to a blender (grinder). Make sure that the mixture turns into a fine powder.
    6.Allow it to cool.
    7.Transfer the powdered mixture into an airtight container.



    HEALTHY TIPS:

    1. Almonds is high in fat and proteins, calcium, and magnesium
    2. 1/4th of Horse Gram contains protein, a good source of iron, calcium, molybdenum
